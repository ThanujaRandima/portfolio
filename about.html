My Quick Story

Hi, I’m Thanuja Randima, a self-taught developer passionate about coding and technology.

When I first began, I faced challenges like “Where do I start?”, “Which language should I learn first?”, “What are the best resources?” Determined to find answers, I immersed myself in learning by watching videos, listening to podcasts, and reading blogs.

With a foundational understanding of HTML, I decided to specialize in Web Development. Choosing between Frontend and Backend, I focused on mastering frontend development first.

I started with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (including ES6 features). Since then, I’ve been continually learning and building projects that expand my skills.

I’m driven by curiosity, creativity, and the desire to craft user-friendly web experiences. I’m excited to explore new tools, frameworks, and opportunities in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
